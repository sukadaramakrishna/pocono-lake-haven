Welcome to the Haven

A Retreat Where Nature Does the Work

Nestled among ancient pines in Pennsylvania's Pocono Mountains, Pocono Lake Haven sits directly on a pristine, private lake. Wake to misty mornings on the water. Spend afternoons kayaking, fishing, or reading on the wraparound deck as herons glide past.

As evening falls, gather around the stone fireplace with family and friends. This is a place where time slows, connections deepen, and memories are made to last a lifetime.

What Makes It Special

Property Highlights

Every detail has been thoughtfully curated to offer comfort, beauty, and unforgettable experiences in the heart of the Poconos.

🌊

Private Lake Access

Step off the deck onto your own private dock. Swim, fish, kayak, or simply watch the sun paint the water gold at dusk. The lake is all yours.

🔥

Stone Fireplace

A grand, floor-to-ceiling stone fireplace anchors the living room, filling the space with warmth and amber glow on cool mountain nights.

🌲

Wraparound Deck

A sweeping deck offers panoramic views of the lake and forest — the perfect setting for morning coffee, sunset cocktails, or star-filled evenings.

🛶

Kayaks & Paddleboards

Complimentary kayaks and paddleboards are ready for your adventure. Explore every quiet cove and mirror-still corner of the lake.

🍳

Chef's Kitchen

A fully equipped, open-concept kitchen with high-end appliances and a large island — ready for everything from big family breakfasts to candlelit dinners.

🌙

Stargazing Deck

Far from city lights, the Poconos offer some of the darkest skies in the Northeast. Lie back and watch the Milky Way arc overhead in full splendor.
